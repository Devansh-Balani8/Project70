campus life

swimming pools

was established in 1994

Eicher School has 3 pools for all age groups.The smallest of 1.2 feet,medium of 2.3 feet,and the last of 3.7,3.11 and 5.6 feet.There are two options- one for only using the pool and other for training and using the pool(obviously).We can use the pools all year because there is an heater in the pool,so we don't have to worry about winters.



library

was established in 1997

The library of eicher school is very big.There are chairs and tables,bean bags,sofas and small beds on the ground.The teachers/librarian only allow 6th grade or above children to take their books to read from theirselves and take a book for 1 week,but the children below 6th grade will be given some books from teachers like dogman,dairy of a wimpy kid and cannot take books home.
